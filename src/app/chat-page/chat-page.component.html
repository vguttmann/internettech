Welcome to Drone Finder Application

Purpose of the Application:

Welcome to the Drone Finder Application, a project developed as part of our university coursework. This application is designed to help you find the perfect drone that suits your needs and preferences. Whether you're new to drones or an experienced pilot, our interactive chatbot guides you through a series of questions to recommend the most suitable drone model for you.

About the Application:

This application serves as a platform where users can interact with the Drone Finder Chatbot to discover drones based on their specific requirements. The chatbot takes into account factors such as application, budget, automation and desired features to provide optimal recommendations.
